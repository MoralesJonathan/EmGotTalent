 ABOUT THE COMPETITION 

Welcome to the third annual EveryMundo’s Got Talent competition! This year's showcase will take place on Friday, July 12th. Here all Mundos are welcome to show us what hidden talents they’ve been waiting to show off. For the top 3 places in the competition there will be rewarding prizes!

  • 1st place: Lunch with the company card!
  • 2nd place: Some cashews from the company room
  • 3rd place: Your very own mug from the kitchen

EveryMundo’s Got Talent was founded 97 years ago on the foundation that we know every man, woman, child, and animal has talent. We here at EveryMundo’s Got Talent wanted to give the people a chance to express their talents to all the Mundos out there, and even earn a chance to win a compelling prize for their effort.
