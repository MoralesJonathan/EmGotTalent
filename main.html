 ABOUT THE COMPETITION 

Welcome to the first annual EveryMundo’s Got Talent competition! This year's showcase will take place on Friday, July 12th. Here all Mundos are welcome to show us what hidden talents they’ve been waiting to show off. For the top 3 places in the competition there will be rewarding prizes!

  • 1st place: Lunch with the company card!
  • 2nd place: Limited Edition custom EGT 2019 Mug.
  • 3rd place: Some cashews from the company room

EveryMundo's Got Talent (often abbreviated as EGT) is a non-televised American talent show competition, and is not part of the global Got Talent franchise created by Simon Cowell. EveryMundo’s Got Talent was founded on the foundation that we know every man, woman, child, and animal has talent. EveryMundo’s Got Talent wanted to give the people a chance to express their talents to all the Mundos out there, and even earn a chance to win a compelling prize for their effort.
